The Seven Plays in English Verse by Sophocles

Electra by Sophocles

Electra

Father, avenger of your own blood, give me strength!

Chorus

Take courage, daughter. The gods will see justice done.

_Electra kneels before the altar, raising her hands to the sky._

Orestes

The time has come to strike down those who wronged us!

Chorus

Justice awaits, and you will bring it forth.

Electra

I will not rest until the blood debt is paid!

_Orestes moves towards the palace with silent determination._
